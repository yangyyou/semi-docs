## S7,F24  Formatted Process Program Acknowledge (FPA)

### Direction

S,H<->E

### Description

Acknowledges reception of a formatted process program at its destination and whether the process program was accepted by the interpreter. A returned status of “accepted” by the interpreter means only that the message is understood. The validity of the contents of the process program is determined through a separate transaction (S7,F27/S7,F28).
