## S7,F20  Current EPPD Data (RED)

### Direction

M,H<-E

### Description

This message is used to transmit the current EPPD.

### Structure

```text
L,n         (number of process programs in the directory)
  1. <PPID1> 
  . 
  . 
  n. <PPIDn> 

```
