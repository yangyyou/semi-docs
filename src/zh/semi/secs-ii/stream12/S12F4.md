## S12,F4  Map Set-up Data (MSD)

### Direction

S,H->E

### Description

Used to send all of the map set-up data required to link the data map with the physical wafer.

### Structure

```text
L,15 
   1. <MID> 
   2. <IDTYP> 
   3. <FNLOC> 
   4. <ORLOC> 
   5. <RPSEL> 
   6. L,n 
        1. <REFPxREFPy> 
        . 
        . 
        n. <REFPxREFPy> 
   7. <DUTMS> 
   8. <XDIES> 
   9. <YDIES> 
  10. <ROWCT> 
  11. <COLCT> 
  12. <PRDCT> 
  13. <BCEQU> 
  14. <NULBC> 
  15. <MLCL> 
```
