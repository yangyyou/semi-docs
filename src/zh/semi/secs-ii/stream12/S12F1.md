## S12,F1  Map Set-up Data Send (MSDS)

### Direction

S,H<-E,reply

### Description

Used to send all of the map set-up data common to all formats and required to link the data map with the physical wafer.

### Structure

```text
L,15 
   1. <MID> 
   2. <IDTYP> 
   3. <FNLOC> 
   4. <FFROT> 
   5. <ORLOC> 
   6. <RPSEL> 
   7. L,n 
        1. <REFPx REFPy> 
        . 
        . 
        n. <REFPx REFPy> 
   8. <DUTMS> 
   9. <XDIES> 
  10. <YDIES> 
  11. <ROWCT> 
  12. <COLCT> 
  13. <NULBC> 
  14. <PRDCT> 
  15. <PRAXI>
```
