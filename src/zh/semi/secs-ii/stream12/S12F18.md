## S12,F18  Map Data Type 3 (MD3)

### Direction

M,H->E

### Description

Used to send map data from the host to the equipment in cartesian coordinate format. Bin values may or may not be included.

### Structure

```text
L,3 
  1. <MID> 
  2. <IDTYP> 
  3. L,n 
       1. L,2 
            1. <XYPOSx1 XYPOSy1> 
            2. <BINLT1...> 
       2. L,2 
       . 
       . 
       n. L,2 
            1. <XYPOSn> 
            2. <BINLTn> 
```

::: info Exception

A zero-length list returned means no such MID.

:::
