## S2,F30 Equipment Constant Namelist (ECN)

### Direction

M,H<-E

### Description

Data Response.

### Structure

```text
L,n (number of equipment constants) 
  1. L,6 
       1. <ECID1> 
       2. <ECNAME1> 
       3. <ECMIN1> 
       4. <ECMAX1> 
       5. <ECDEF1> 
       6. <UNITS1> 
  2. L,6 
  . 
  . 
  n. L,6 
       1. <ECIDn> 
       2. <ECNAMEn> 
       3. <ECMINn> 
       4. <ECMAXn> 
       5. <ECDEFn> 
       6. <UNITSn> 
```

::: info Exception
A zero-length list means send information for all ECIDs.
:::
