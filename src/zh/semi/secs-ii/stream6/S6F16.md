## S6,F16  Event Report Data (ERD)

### Direction

M,H<-E

### Description

Equipment sends reports linked to given CEID to host.

### Structure

```text
L,3 
  1. <DATAID> 
  2. <CEID> 
  3. L,a 
       1. L,2 
            1. <RPTID1> 
            2. L,b 
                 1. <V1> 
                 . 
                 . 
                 b. <Vb> 
            . 
            . 
       a. L,2           report a
            1. <RPTIDa> 
            2. L,c      #Vs this report 
                 1. <V1> 
                 . 
                 . 
                 c. <Vc>
```

::: info Exception

A zero-length item means there are no reports linked to the given CEID.

:::
