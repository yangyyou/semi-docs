## S6,F8   Data Transfer Data (DDD)

### Direction

M,H<-E

### Description

Equipment sends data to the host.

### Structure

```text
L,3 
  1. <DATAID> 
  2. <CEID> 
  3. L,n 
       1. L,2 
            1. <DSID1> 
            2. L,m 
                 1. L,2 
                      1. <DVNAME1> 
                      2. <DVVAL1> 
                 2. L,2 
                 . 
                 . 
                 m. L,2 
                      1. <DVNAMEm> 
                      2. <DVVALm> 
       2. L,2 
       . 
       . 
       n. L,2 
            1. <DSIDn> 
            2. etc.
```

::: info Exception

A zero-length item returned means the requested data cannot be sent.

:::
