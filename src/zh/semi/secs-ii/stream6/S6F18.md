## S6,F18  Annotated Event Report Data (AERD)

### Direction

M,H<-E

### Description

Equipment sends annotated reports linked to given CEID.

### Structure

```text
L,3 
  1. <DATAID> 
  2. <CEID> 
  3. L,a 
       1. L,2 
            1. <RPTID1> 
            2. L,b 
                 1. L,2 
                      1. <VID1> 
                 . 
                 . 
                 b. L,2 
                      1. <VIDb> 
                      b. <Vb> 
       . 
       . 
       a. L,2 
            1. <RPTIDa> 
            2. L,c 
                 1. L,2 
                      1. <VID1> 
                      2. <V1> 
                 . 
                 . 
                 c. L,2 
                      1. <VIDc> 
                      2. <Vc> 
```

::: info Exception

If there are no reports linked to the event a ‘null’ report is assumed. A zero-length list for # of reports means there are no reports linked to the given CEID.

:::
