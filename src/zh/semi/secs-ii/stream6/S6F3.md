## S6,F3  Discrete Variable Data Send (DVS)

### Direction

M,H<-E,[reply]

### Description

Any data report which is initiated by an event, such as the completion of a measurement, rather than passage of time is called a discrete variable. Some equipment may have several possible events on which to send the data. S2,F15 is used to select the desired reporting events. Reports requiring only one block of data may report directly to the host with this message. If S6,F3 is multi-block, it must be preceded by the S6,F5/S6,F6 Inquire/Grant transaction.

### Structure

```text
L,3 
  1. <DATAID> 
  2. <CEID> 
  3. L,n 
       1. L,2 
            1. <DSID1> 
            2. L,m 
                 1. L,2 
                      1. <DVNAME1> 
                      2. <DVVAL1> 
                 2. L,2 
                 . 
                 . 
                 m. L,2 
                      1. <DVNAMEm> 
                      2. <DVVALm> 
       2. L,2 
       . 
       . 
       n. L,2 
            1. <DSIDn> 
            2. etc.
```
