## S1,F12  Status Variable Namelist Reply (SVNRR)

### Direction

M,H<-E

### Description

The equipment reports to the host the name and units of the requested SVs.

### Structure

```text
L,n 
  1. L,3 
       1. <SVID1> 
       2. <SVNAME1> 
       3. <UNITS1> 
  2. L,3 
  . 
  . 
  n. L,3 
       1. <SVIDn> 
       2. <SVNAMEn> 
       3. <UNITSn>
```

::: info Exception
Zero-length ASCII items for both SVNAMEi and UNITSi indicates that the SVID does not exist.
:::
