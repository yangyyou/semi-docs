## S16,F4  Process Job Create Acknowledge (PRJCA)

### Direction

S,H<-E

### Description

Acknowledge or report error in the creation of a Process Job.

### Structure

```text
L,2 
  1. <PRJOBID> 
  2. L,2 
       1. <ACKA> 
       2. L,n 
            1. L,2 
                 1. <ERRCODE1> 
                 2. <ERRTEXT1> 
            . 
            . 
            . 
            n. L,2 
                 1. <ERRCODEn> 
                 2. <ERRTEXTn>
```

::: info Exception

This list may be zero length, generally the case when ACKA indicates success. When ACKA indicates a create failure, the equipment may supply one or more ERRCODEs.

:::
