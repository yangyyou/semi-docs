## S16,F3  Process Job Create Request (PRJCR)

### Direction

M,H->E,reply

### Description

The purpose of this message is to request material to be processed on a Process Module.

### Structure

```text
L,5 
  1. <DATAID> 
  2. <MF> 
  3. L,n 
       1. <MID1> 
       . 
       . 
       n. <MIDn> 
  4. L,3 
       1. <PRRECIPEMETHOD> 
       2. <RCPSPEC> 
       3. L,m         (m = {c,2}) 
            1. L,2 
                 1. <RCPPARNM1> 
                 2. <RCPPARVAL1> 
            . 
            . 
            m. L,2 
                 1. <RCPPARNMm> 
                 2. <RCPPARVALm> 
  5. <PRPROCESSSTART> 
```
