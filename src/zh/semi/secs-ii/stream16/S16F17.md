## S16,F17  PRJobDequeue

### Direction

M,H->E,reply

### Description

Used to remove process jobs from the equipment for jobs that have not begun processing.

### Structure

```text
L,m                 [m = # jobs to remove]
  1. <PRJOBID1> 
  . 
  . 
  m. <PRJOBIDm> 
```

::: info Exception

If m = 0, then de-queue all.

:::
