## S16,F1  Multi-block Process Job Data Inquire (PRJI)

### Direction

S,H<->E,reply

### Description

If any of Processing Management messages are larger than one block, then this transaction must precede that message.

### Structure

```text
L,2 
  1. <DATAID> 
  2. <DATALENGTH>
```
