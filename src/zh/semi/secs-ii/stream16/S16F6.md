## S16,F6  Process Job Command Acknowledge (PRJCMDA)

### Direction

S,H<-E

### Description

The processing service sends its confirmation for receipt of a command request.

### Structure

```text
L,2 
   1. <PRJOBID> 
   2. L,2 
        1. <ACKA> 
        2. L,n                (n = {0,n}) 
             1. L,2 
                  1. <ERRCODE1> 
                  2. <ERRTEXT1> 
             . 
             . 
             n. L,2 
                  1. <ERRCODEn> 
                  2. <ERRTEXTn> 
```

::: info Exception

This list n may be zero length.

:::
